Nadav Cohen

I'm a Computer Science Ph.D. student at Reichman University, working under the supervision of Prof. Ariel Shamir.

My research focuses on generative models for 2D and 3D applications, with a particular interest in developing algorithm-driven art forms and advancing 3D tools for the game and animation industries.

Beyond research, I enjoy painting, nature photography, gaming, and spending time with family and friends.
